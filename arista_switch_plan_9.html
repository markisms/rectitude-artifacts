MLAG Design
Active/standby MLAG — each IDF has one 10GBase uplink to Core-A and one to Core-B. Traffic flows over primary; standby assumes on core failure. Core pair operates as a single MLAG domain.
Uplink Speed
10GBase — SFP-10G-LR (SM) or SFP-10G-SR (MM) in the 7050SX3-24YC4C's SFP28 ports at full speed. Copper access ports remain 1G RJ45. Upgrade to 25G: swap SFP-10G-LR/SR transceivers for SFP-25G-LR/SR — no recabling, no switch hardware change.
Fiber Plant
12 Phase 1 IDFs: 8 single mode (SFP-10G-LR) + 4 legacy multimode (SFP-10G-SR, confirmed functional). Phase 2: all SM. MM→SM optic swap at Phase 2 — no switch hardware change.
